preparation time

  • Total: approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minute
  • cooking: 5 minutes

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

    1. Beat teh egg: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until the are well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
    2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over madium heat and add butter or oil.
    3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted aand bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
    4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, spinkle you chosen fillings over one half of onto a plate.
    5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and flod it over the fillings, let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
    6. Enjoy: Server hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
